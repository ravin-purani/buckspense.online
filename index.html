Privacy-First Expense Management

Buckspense is built on the belief that your personal financial details belong to you alone. That's why we built our core tracker to run 100% offline.

100% Offline & Secure

Operates fully offline on your Android device. Your ledgers are saved locally using sandboxed storage—no external cloud servers, no databases leaks.

Ultra Lightweight UI

Zero clutter. Open the app, type your amount, select a category, and save. Enjoy immediate loading speeds and dynamic local budget analytics.

Completely Free & Ad-Free

Built as a pure personal utility. No recurring subscription limits, no behind-the-scenes premium blocks, and no distracting banners or pop-up ads.
