Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 25, 2026 Version: 1.0.0

1. Introduction

Welcome to buckspense.online (referred to as "we", "us", "our", or "Buckspense"). We are deeply committed to protecting your personal information and your right to financial privacy.

This Privacy Policy details how we collect, process, utilize, and protect your information when you access our dashboard, tools, and website application. Please read this document carefully to fully understand our data practices. If you disagree with any terms inside this policy, please discontinue access to our services immediately.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when registering for the early developer beta, subscribing to updates, or configuring dashboard settings:

  • Personal Data: We collect contact identifiers such as your name, email address, and billing credentials when you opt-in or sign up.
  • Financial Metadata: If you manually upload or connect spreadsheets, transaction lists, budgets, or cash flows, we collect that transaction metadata purely to structure your interactive dashboard charts.
  • Device & Telemetry Data: We automatically collect certain system indicators such as IP addresses, browser specifications, operating system versions, and page usage logs to ensure premium stability and responsiveness.

3. How We Use Information

We process your information based on legitimate commercial interests, fulfillment of contract templates, and compliance with statutory obligations:

  • To deliver, configure, and maintain your personal dashboard analytics.
  • To send you updates regarding beta milestones, tech releases, or administrative notifications.
  • To ensure the integrity of our systems by monitoring against fraudulent activity or network exploits.
  • To enforce our terms of service and legal regulations.

We **never** sell, rent, or trade your personal financial transaction history or email records to any third-party marketing companies.

4. Data Security

Security is the absolute core of our product architecture. We have implemented robust technical and organizational security controls designed to safeguard any financial data we process:

All connections to our dashboard use SSL/TLS encryption transit methods. At rest, sensitive database records are secured with banking-grade AES-256 standard encryption keys. However, please remember that no electronic transmission over the internet can be guaranteed 100% secure, so please ensure you use secure passwords and do not share your private credentials.

5. Your Legal Rights

Depending on your local jurisdiction (such as rights granted under the GDPR or CCPA), you hold specific rights regarding your personal and financial datasets:

  • Right to Access: You can request complete export files of all data registered under your profile.
  • Right to Rectification: You can edit or correct any information through your settings drawer.
  • Right to Erasure (Deletion): You can completely purge all records from our servers at any time, instantly.
